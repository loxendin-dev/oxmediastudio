The Conversion
Layer Method™

A purpose-built page that sits between your ad and your website. It prepares visitors emotionally, reinforces your ad's promise, and drives one clear action — no distractions, no menus, no exits.

The page immediately matches the ad's language and offer. Visitors feel they arrived in the right place — and stay long enough to act.
