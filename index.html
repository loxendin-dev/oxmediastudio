The Conversion
Layer Method™

A purpose-built page that sits between your ad and your website. It prepares visitors emotionally, reinforces your ad's promise, and drives one clear action — no distractions, no menus, no exits.

Step 01

Reinforce the Ad Promise

The page immediately matches the ad's language and offer. Visitors feel they arrived in the right place — and stay long enough to act.

Step 02

Emotion-first copy amplifies the desire or pain that drove the click. The visitor stays engaged and moves naturally toward action.

Step 03

Establish Trust Fast

Reviews, testimonials, credentials, and guarantees appear before the visitor has a chance to second-guess their decision.
